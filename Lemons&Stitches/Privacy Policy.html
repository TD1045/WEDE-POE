Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 27 August 2025

At Lemons & Stitches, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following personal information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Billing and Shipping Address
  • Payment Information (processed securely via third-party gateways)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To fulfill and manage orders
  • To communicate with you (e.g., order updates, marketing)
  • To personalize your shopping experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with our website. You can control cookie usage through your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with trusted third-party services (e.g., payment processors and shipping providers) only to fulfill your order or comply with the law.

5. Data Security

Your information is securely stored and encrypted where appropriate. Payment data is processed through secure third-party platforms and never stored on our servers.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Contact us at ST10379134@IMCONNECT.EDU.ZA to make a request.
